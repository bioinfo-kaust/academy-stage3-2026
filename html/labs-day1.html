Day 1: Hands-on Labs

L1: Warm-up for Large-scale Analysis & Genomic File Formats

Lab Instructions: L1

In this lab, you will get familiarized with the training environment, learn to access public genomics resources, and download example RNA-seq datasets from public repositories.

Steps:
  1. Familiarization with the training environment
  2. Access public genomics data resources
  3. Download example RNA-seq datasets from public repositories

L2: Hands-on with Transcriptomics Data

Lab Instructions: L2

This lab focuses on hands-on experience with transcriptomics data, where you will execute bioinformatics tools and explore sequencing data.

Steps:
  1. Execute bioinformatics tools and explore sequencing data

L3: Project and Dataset Selection

Lab Instructions: L3

In this lab, you will form teams, pick a project topic, and search for and download relevant public datasets and associated metadata.

Steps:
  1. Form teams and pick a project topic.
  2. Search for and download relevant public datasets and associated metadata.
